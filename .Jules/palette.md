## 2024-07-04 - Associate Form Labels with Inputs
**Learning:** Found that many `<label>` elements in the settings and automation modals were missing `for="..."` attributes, preventing proper association with their respective `<input>`, `<select>`, and `<textarea>` elements. This is a crucial accessibility issue as it affects screen reader usability and reduces the click target area for users.
**Action:** Always ensure that every `<label>` tag explicitly references the `id` of its associated form control using the `for` attribute. This should be a standard practice for all new form elements added to the UI.
